Calculation Methodology

How we determine the minimum economically sustainable price.

1. The Formula

The minimum price ($P_{min}$) is calculated to cover production costs, guarantee a profit margin, and automatically adjust for quality and climate risks.

Step 1: Base_Cost = (Production_Costs + Cert_Defaults) / Marketable_Yield

Step 2: Cost_w_Margin = Base_Cost √ó (1 + Margin_%)

P_min = Cost_w_Margin + Quality_Adjustment + Risk_Premium

* The final price is always the greater of P_min or the Consortium Floor Price.
